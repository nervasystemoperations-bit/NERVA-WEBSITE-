How NERVA Works

Every action is an event.
Every event is traceable.

NERVA runs on a strict event-driven pipeline. Nothing is silent. Nothing is manual. Each step is logged, validated, and reported in real time.

01

Event Triggered

Every workflow starts with a trigger — an inbound call, a form submission, a webhook from your POS, or a scheduled event. NERVA listens 24/7 across every channel you operate on.

Inbound Call
→ Voice AI activated
Form Submit
→ Lead pipeline started
POS Webhook
→ Order engine triggered
Scheduled
→ Broadcast campaign fired
02

Validated & Classified

Before any action is taken, NERVA validates the incoming data for integrity, deduplication, and routing logic. AI classifies intent and assigns a priority score in milliseconds.

Data Check
→ Schema validation passed
Intent AI
→ Classified: High-intent lead
Lead Score
→ 94 / 100 — route to priority
Dedup
→ New contact confirmed
03

Automated Action

The right action fires automatically — a follow-up SMS, a booking confirmation, a CRM update, an order acknowledgment. No human required. No delay. No mistake.

SMS Sent
→ "Thanks for calling, here's your link"
CRM Updated
→ Contact + notes + score written
Calendar
→ Appointment blocked & confirmed
Notification
→ Owner alerted if human needed
04

Logged & Reported

Every event, action, and outcome is written to your database in real time. Your KPI dashboard updates automatically. You always know exactly what happened and when.

Audit Log
→ Full event trace stored
KPI Update
→ Dashboard refreshed live
Analytics
→ Revenue attribution updated
Report
→ Weekly summary queued
